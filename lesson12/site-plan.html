Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to promote friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs and to provide information about the services, requirements, and rental process.

Audience

The audience for the website is every person or customer that like to do their homework before getting to the island. Also, the audience is everyone who is interested to receive information about the services, requirements, and rental process about motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs. Although the information if for everyone, the requirements to get the services is that you should be at least 16 years old.
