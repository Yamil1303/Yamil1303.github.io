Header and Footer

For these elements we I will use the primary color as a background and the Accent1 for the color of the letters. An example of this is this same website plan you are in.

Paragraphs

For the normal paragraphs I will use just the Accent1 with a background color of the accent1 in order for it to have contrast.

Menu

For the menu and the items I will use the color from Accent1 for the color of the letters and Accent2 for the backgrpund. Also, when the mouse is over the items, the color will be now be Primary and the background will be Accent1
