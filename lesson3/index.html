Purpose

Life is busy and full of responsabilities. Imagine that one day you are getting ready for work or some meeting, and as you go out you approach your destiny it starts to rain. The purpose of this wensite is for everyone to prevent such things. It is better to be informed and know what the weather will be like next week, tomorrow, and even later. So, with this, people will enjoy more their days and even will take advantage of the weather to do some fun activity out there.
