Typography

Heading Font: ['Share Tech', sans-serif]

I will use 'Share Tech', sans-serif as the font family for all my headings from h1 to h6.

Paragraph Font: ['Cormorant Garamond', serif]

Every word in a paragraph will use the 'Cormorant Garamond', serif font. I think that the effect it has for the letter is fancy and will help the style of the website.
