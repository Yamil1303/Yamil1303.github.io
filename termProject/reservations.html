Rental Agreement

Before you go ahead and complete the form for the reservation of the scooter, you should know that you are agreeing with the terms and conditions of the contract. Such terms and conditions are as follow:

  • We may take the scooter back if it is abandoned or placed inappropriately.
  • You should pick up the scooter on the same date and time that is specified. If it is not possible for any external reason, you should call and renew the reservation at least 6 hours before the original time.
  • You should return the scooter in the same conditions you received it.
  • You are responsable if the scooter is stolen or get lost.
  • You should return the scooter by the agreed time.
  • You are responsable for any damage the scooter receives since the time you pick it up until you return it to the agreed place.

You will have to face the justice imposed by the police officers of the city in case of any violation to any point of the agreement. You will have to pay the price for any damage or loss of the property as well as any penalty fee if the scooter is not returned on time. Being aware of this rental agreement, you can go ahead and fill the form to make your reservation.
